VastuLuo — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

VastuLuo ("the App") is a Feng Shui and Vastu Shastra geomantic advisor. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

Data We Access

1. Compass / Motion Sensors

The App reads your device's compass and motion sensors to detect your facing direction.

Processed locally Never sent to servers Never stored

2. Location (Optional)

If you grant permission, the App accesses your approximate location solely to calculate magnetic declination for compass accuracy.

Used only for calculation Never stored Never shared

3. AI Chat Messages (Premium Feature)

When you use the AI Advisor feature, your messages are sent to Anthropic's API for processing. These messages are not stored by the App. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their own privacy policy.

4. Purchase Status

Your Premium purchase status is stored locally on your device using the platform's secure storage. No account or login is required.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or any personal information
  • Analytics, usage tracking, or behavior data
  • Advertising identifiers or device fingerprints
  • Photos, contacts, or files
  • Cookies or web tracking

Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Play (for purchases and distribution) and Anthropic's Claude API (for the AI feature). No other third-party services, analytics tools, or ad networks are used.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. The App contains no ads, no social features, and no data collection.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is no personal data at risk. All compass and location data is processed on your device and never leaves it.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
